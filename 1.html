Bench Press

What is bench press good for?
Bench press can be used to increase strength, improve endurance, and tome the muscles of the upper body, including the chest, arms, amd shoulders.

In short, you will not build significant muscle mass in your biceps by doing the Bench Press.
However, you biceps act as a stabilisation muscle that helps to perform the press,
